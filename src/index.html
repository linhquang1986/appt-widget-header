Usage

  1. Include appt-widget-header.js.
  2. Add appt-widget-header-directive as a dependency to your app.
  3. Make <appt-widget-header>s.

In action

Heute, Montag 7.Juli - Ihre Termine 


<div ng-app="appt-widget-header-directive">
          <appt-widget-header> Heute, Montag 7.Juli - Ihre Termine </appt-widget-header>
          </div>
